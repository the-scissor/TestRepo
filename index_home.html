Resurgence

Welcome to Resurgence, the pursuit of grandeur, opulence, creativity and perfection. Be ready to wander among unfathomable lands, lands gleaming with embellishing lights, fragrant ambiance, mesmerizing performances, pulsating drumbeats, electrifying guitar chords,tantalizing quizzes and unforgettable memories. The Techno-Cultural Delight puts forth an unparalleled multitude of events, meticulously fashioned to suit the interests of computer geeks, gaming freaks, technolics, bibliophilics, music and dance maniacs and everyone else.
